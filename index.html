What is Omniscient?

Omniscient is a simple program that takes the currently active window's title and sets it as your rich presence on Discord.

Interested?

You can download Omniscient by clicking on the button on the top right corner.
The currently compatible apps list is on the Info page.
(Most apps do work with Omniscient, these are just the apps that have an icon for them that shows up on discord.)

Lightweight
Omniscient doesn't need alot of resources to run properly.
It just works.
(Like maybe 75% of the time.)
Doesn't collect data
Omniscient doesn't collect user data, or sell it to Google or Facebook. How sad.
