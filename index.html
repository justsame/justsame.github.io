Omniscient Rich Presence

Omniscient is a simple program that takes the currently active window's title and sets it as your Rich Presence on Discord.

Supported apps list is on the Information page.


Now with Incognito Mode and a word blacklist!

Because not everything needs to be shared through Omniscient.
